Mission Statement

We're worth more than we know.

Like most industries, advertising’s top earners are hetero, white, and male. By intention or incidence, people of color and women are paid less for doing the same work as their white male peers.

Real Agency Salaries was launched in 2019 as a response to this pay inequity. We equip creative agency talent with the salary info and skills they need to better understand their worth and negotiate fair pay for themselves.

Supported by voluntary and confidential submissions from advertising talent globally, RAS is a peer-to-peer compensation transparency platform removing the stigma around discussing how much we make, with the goal of permanently closing the wage gap.
